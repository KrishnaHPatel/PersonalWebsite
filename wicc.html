Key Initiatives & Impact

Major programs and events I've led to strengthen our community

Mentorship Program Expansion

Expanded our mentorship program to serve more students, creating meaningful connections between upperclassmen and underclassmen. Organized mentor-mentee matching events and provided ongoing support to ensure successful relationships.

Allyship Workshops

Boosted allyship workshops to educate the broader Cornell community about supporting women and gender minorities in computing. These workshops help create a more inclusive environment across campus.

Community-Building Events

Organized and executed various community events including G-Bodies, socials, and volunteer opportunities. These events unite our community and create lasting friendships among members.
