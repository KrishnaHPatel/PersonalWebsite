Founder of STEAM 4 Girls

  • Initiated STEAM 4 Girls, a program to encourage young girls to explore and excel in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math.
  • Developed the curriculum, managed outreach to local schools and organizations, and created tutorials and demonstration videos to assist with learning.
  • Organized and led workshops, mentorship sessions, and hands-on activities to bridge the gender gap in STEAM fields.
  • Ran the program for over 40+ young girls, forming long-lasting connections and inspiring them to pursue STEM careers.
  • Provided guidance and support to participants, helping them build confidence and develop essential skills in STEAM areas.
